# FieldReport — Superintendent One-Pager

Welcome. This app lets you walk the site, snap photos, and say a quick note for each one —
**even with no signal** — and turns it into a clean, sendable field report. Takes 2 minutes
to set up. You only do the setup once.

---

## ① Install it on your phone (one time, ~1 minute)

**You must add it to your Home Screen** — that's what lets it hold your photos safely with no
signal. Don't just use it in the browser.

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open this link in **Safari** (not Chrome): **`<CAPTURE_APP_URL>`**
2. Tap the **Share** button — the square with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down the gray menu and tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. Tap **"Add"** (top right).
5. Close Safari. Open **FieldReport** from the new icon on your Home Screen. ✅

**On Android (Chrome):** open the link → tap the **⋮** menu → **"Add to Home screen"** → **Add.**

> If the app shows a screen telling you to add it to the Home Screen, that just means it was
> opened in the browser instead of from the icon — follow the steps above and open it from the icon.

---

## ② Walk the site (works with NO signal)

Open FieldReport from the Home Screen icon. For each thing you want in the report:

1. Tap **New Observation.**
2. **Take a photo** (or a few) of what you're looking at.
3. Tap **Record**, say your note out loud — plain talk is fine ("drywall's hung on three,
   they're taping the corridors") — then tap **Stop.**
4. Tap **Save.** It's now stored safely on the phone. Repeat for the next thing.

You'll see a running list of everything you've captured, with a count. You can **retake** or
**delete** anything before you send. It's fine if the phone locks, you take a call, or you lose
signal in a basement — **nothing is lost.**

---

## ③ Send it when you're back on signal/Wi-Fi

1. When you have a good connection, open FieldReport.
2. Tap **Sync.** Watch the progress bar fill as it uploads. If something hiccups, tap **Retry** —
   it picks up where it left off.
3. When it finishes, you'll get a **review link** (or find it in the office).

---

## ④ Review and send (this part is on a computer or your phone browser)

1. Open the **review link**. Give it a minute the first time — it's transcribing and writing up your notes.
2. Read it over. **Tap any text to fix it** — it saves as you type. (If it wrote "poured" but you
   said "prepped," fix it here. Your name's on this.)
3. Tap **Finalize.** You'll get a **web link** to send to the PM/owner and a **PDF** to download.

The footer always says "AI-assisted, superintendent-reviewed" — because you reviewed it.
